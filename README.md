# Portfolio Artifact Submission – Weight Tracker App (Project Three)

## Reflection

### Briefly summarize the requirements and goals of the app you developed. What user needs was this app designed to address?

The goal of my app was to give users a simple way to track their weight and view progress over time. It was designed to address the need for an easy and organized tool for personal health tracking.

### What screens and features were necessary to support user needs and produce a user-centered UI for the app? How did your UI designs keep users in mind? Why were your designs successful?

The app included login, a main dashboard, and screens to enter and view weight data. I kept the design simple and easy to navigate so users could complete tasks without extra steps. I think the designs were successful because they focused on usability and clarity.

### How did you approach the process of coding your app? What techniques or strategies did you use? How could those techniques or strategies be applied in the future?

I built the app one feature at a time and tested as I went. This helped me stay organized and made it easier to fix problems. I can use the same step-by-step process in future projects.

### How did you test to ensure your code was functional? Why is this process important, and what did it reveal?

I tested navigation, input handling, and feature behavior during development. Testing was important because it helped me catch issues early and improve how the app worked. It also showed where the app flow needed adjustments.

### Consider the full app design and development process from initial planning to finalization. Where did you have to innovate to overcome a challenge?

A challenge was making sure the app flow and features worked smoothly together while keeping the app simple. I adjusted the design and logic as I tested to improve usability.

### In what specific component of your mobile app were you particularly successful in demonstrating your knowledge, skills, and experience?

I was most successful in building the overall app structure and connecting the main features in a way that supports the user’s goals.
